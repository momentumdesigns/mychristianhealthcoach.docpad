Welcome to your blank canvas for DocPad

To best use this, follow these steps

  1. Download Sublime Text 2
  2. Open it up, press view, layout, rows 3
  3. Put your src/documents/index.html.md and src/layouts/default.html.coffee in your top row
  4. Put your src/documents/styles/style.css.styl in the second row
  5. Put your src/documents/scripts/script.js.coffee in the third row

This is insanely awesome as we also have docpad's autoupdate plugin enabled. So whenever you make a change, your web browser will notice and refresh :-) How awesome is that!

Later on there will be a nice video here to show you how to utilise all the awesomeness you now have in your possession, for now just refer to the DocPad FAQ
